Superb Recruits is a compatibility add-on that bridges The Recruits Mod and Superb Warfare, allowing your NPC recruits to seamlessly operate and interact with ground vehicles from Superb Warfare.

With this mod, your recruits can now drive, ride, and utilize Superb Warfare’s vehicles just like players do — perfect for creating AI-controlled squads, convoy patrols, or mechanized units in large-scale battles.

Whether you’re leading an army of foot soldiers or commanding a fleet of tanks, Superb Recruits brings your military gameplay to life with smarter, more immersive vehicle integration.
